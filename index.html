A SAFE SPACE FOR ARTISTS TO BE ARTISTS

Founded by noise complaints. The Birch Room is a place for artists to focus on the music. Small, Intimate and Effective. The room is designed to enhance productive creativity.

NOT YOUR UNCLE'S STUDIO

The Birch Room is a small 12 ft x 14 ft room within a 900 Sq ft. creative studio. Designed with the producer, engineer and vocalist in mind we value creativity and flow state the most. The space is tailored towards individual artists and smaller collaborator groups. A plug and play system allows you to simply bring just your laptop or all your Midi gear. Up to you on what you want to carry up the stairs.
